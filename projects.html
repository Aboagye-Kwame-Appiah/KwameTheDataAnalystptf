02
Manufacturing · Operations Analytics · Python
Production Efficiency Optimisation & Waste Reduction Analytics
PythonPandasSciPyExcelPower BI
Business Problem
A manufacturing facility faced costly mismatches between planned capacity and actual demand. Excess inventory, underutilised machines, and supply waste were eroding margins with no data-driven mechanism to identify root causes or test corrective scenarios.
Methodology
1. Demand Modelling — extracted and cleaned production schedules vs. actual output
2. Gap Analysis — Python (Pandas) to identify capacity-demand misalignment patterns
3. Optimisation Engine — SciPy-based model to generate corrective production plans
4. Monitoring Layer — Power BI dashboard for real-time efficiency tracking
Key Insights
Production lines 2 and 4 showed highest demand-capacity mismatch, driving 68% of total waste. Shifting scheduling priority to high-demand SKUs while buffering low-frequency lines reduced projected waste. The optimisation model validated corrective action before implementation.

E-Commerce · Power BI · Revenue Analytics
E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
Power BIDAXData Modelling
Business Problem
An e-commerce operator needed a consolidated analytical view across multiple markets, product segments, and sales channels — specifically to understand how discount strategies were affecting profit margins relative to revenue growth.
Methodology
1. Data Model — built star schema in Power BI with fact and dimension tables
2. DAX Measures — calculated margin %, discount impact, and YoY variance
3. Interactive Reports — sliceable reports by market, product, and time
4. Insight Annotations — flagged anomalies and opportunities in report narrative
Key Insights
Heavy discount activity in Market B was driving revenue volume but compressing margins by 11 percentage points. Product category C showed strong margin performance with low promotional spend — signalling a reallocation opportunity.
